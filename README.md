# Restaurant AI Automation Suite

A collection of AI-powered restaurant automation workflows built using n8n, Groq API, JavaScript, and Google Sheets.

---

## Projects Included

### AI Restaurant Review Management

Automates customer review handling by generating AI-powered responses, detecting sentiment, and identifying escalation cases.

**Features**
- Sentiment Analysis
- Automated Review Replies
- Escalation Detection
- Google Sheets Integration

---

### Festival Marketing Campaign Generator

Generates complete festival marketing campaigns for restaurants including offers, social media promotions, and customer engagement plans.

**Features**
- Campaign Generation
- Instagram Content
- WhatsApp Promotions
- Offer Recommendations

---

### AI Food Caption Generator

Creates engaging restaurant marketing content using AI.

**Features**
- Instagram Captions
- Facebook Posts
- Google Business Content
- Hashtag Suggestions

---

## Technology Stack

- n8n
- Groq API
- JavaScript
- Google Sheets
- Prompt Engineering
- Workflow Automation

---

## Business Impact

These workflows help restaurants automate customer engagement, marketing activities, and content generation using AI-powered automation.
